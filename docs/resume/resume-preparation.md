# Resume Preparation

A strong resume can significantly increase shortlist chances.

## Checklist

- One page, clear structure
- Impact-focused bullet points
- Projects with measurable outcomes
- Skills aligned with job descriptions
- No spelling or formatting errors

## Recommended Sections

- Education
- Technical skills
- Projects
- Experience / internships
- Achievements

## Pro Tip

Tailor your resume for every role before applying.
