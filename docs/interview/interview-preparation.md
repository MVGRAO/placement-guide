# Interview Preparation

Interview success comes from revision, communication, and mock practice.

## Preparation Plan

1. Revise DSA, SQL, and core CS daily
2. Practice explaining your projects in 2-3 minutes
3. Conduct mock interviews with friends or seniors
4. Prepare HR answers for common questions

## Common Questions to Prepare

- Tell me about yourself
- Explain your toughest project challenge
- Why should we hire you?
- Questions for interviewer
