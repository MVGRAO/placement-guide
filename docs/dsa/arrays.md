# Arrays Preparation Guide

Arrays are one of the most asked topics in online assessments and interviews.

## What to Learn

- Traversal, prefix sums, sliding window
- Two pointers and sorting-based tricks
- Hashing with arrays
- Binary search on answer

## 4-Week Plan

1. Week 1: Easy array problems and patterns
2. Week 2: Medium-level sliding window and prefix sum
3. Week 3: Mixed revision and timed sets
4. Week 4: Mock interview rounds
